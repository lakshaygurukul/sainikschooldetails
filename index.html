Sainik School Criteria and Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria

1. Nationality

  • Only Indian citizens are eligible to apply.

2. Age Criteria

  • For Class VI Admission:
    • The candidate should be between 10 to 12 years of age as of March 31st of the year of admission.
  • For Class IX Admission:
    • The candidate should be between 13 to 15 years of age as of March 31st of the year of admission.
    • Additionally, the candidate must have passed Class VIII from a recognized school at the time of admission.

3. Gender

  • Both boys and girls are eligible for admission in Class VI.
  • Only boys are eligible for admission in Class IX.

4. Reservation Policy

  • Scheduled Castes (SC): 15% of the seats.
  • Scheduled Tribes (ST): 7.5% of the seats.
  • Children of Service Personnel including Ex-Servicemen: 25% of the seats are reserved for children of service personnel, including ex-servicemen.
  • State Quota: 67% of the seats are reserved for candidates from the state where the Sainik School is located. The remaining 33% are for candidates from other states and union territories.

5. Medical Fitness

  • Candidates must be medically fit as per the standards prescribed by the Sainik Schools Society.
